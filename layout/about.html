Soluwin can be translated to mean "Innovative solution provider" in English.

It is a company that specializes in providing cutting-edge solutions in the field of information and communication technology. The company's name suggests that they are dedicated to delivering unique and effective solutions that help their clients to achieve success in their businesses.
